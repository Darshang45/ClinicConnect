🏥 ClinicConnect – AI-Powered Hospital Management System

ClinicConnect is a scalable, full-stack Hospital Management System (HMS) built using the MERN stack to digitize and streamline hospital operations. The platform provides role-based dashboards for Patients, Doctors, Receptionists, Pharmacists, and Administrators, enabling seamless management of appointments, consultations, prescriptions, pharmacy operations, and hospital administration through a single integrated system.

Patients can book appointments, access medical records, communicate securely with doctors, receive digital prescriptions, and manage their healthcare journey from a personalized dashboard. Doctors can manage consultation schedules, maintain patient records, generate digital prescriptions, and share medical reports securely. Receptionists handle patient registrations and appointment management, while pharmacists can access digital prescriptions, generate invoices, and automatically update medicine inventory. Administrators oversee departments, users, doctors, analytics, and hospital-wide operations.

The system is designed with scalability in mind, supporting multiple departments and doctors while providing secure authentication, real-time notifications, and an extensible architecture. Future enhancements include AI-powered medical report summarization, symptom guidance, prescription analysis, medicine reminders, predictive appointment management, and intelligent healthcare analytics, making ClinicConnect a modern and future-ready healthcare platform.
